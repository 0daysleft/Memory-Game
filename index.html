🧠 Memory Game – Beginner Level

How to Play

  • 🟦 Game Layout: 12 hidden boxes, each with a hidden image. 6 pairs in total.
  • 👆 Your Goal: Click any two boxes to reveal the images. Try to find two that match.
  • ✅ If the images match: You earn a point. The boxes turn green.
  • ❌ If the images don't match: You don’t get a point. The boxes turn red, then reset.
  • 🎯 Win Condition: Match all 6 pairs to complete the level. Use your memory to improve!
  • 🧩 Difficulty: Beginner – great for practicing your memory skills!
